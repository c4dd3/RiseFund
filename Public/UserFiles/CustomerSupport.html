Customer Support

How do I activate notifications?

You can activate notifications by going to Settings and enabling them in the Notifications section.

How do I donate?

You can donate through our platform using PayPal, credit card, or other payment methods available on the Donations page.

Requirements to publish my project

To publish your project, you need to submit an application with details about your project, and it must meet our guidelines.

How do I request refunds?

Refund requests can be made by contacting our support team within 14 days of the transaction.
